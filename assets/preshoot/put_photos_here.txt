Drop your pre-shoot photos here, named exactly:

    capture_1.jpg
    capture_2.jpg
    capture_3.jpg

The website already has elegant "Photo coming soon" placeholders showing
in the Captured Moments section — the moment you add a file with the
correct name, that placeholder is replaced by your real photo automatically.
No other changes needed — just refresh the page after adding files.

TIPS
─────────────────────────────────────────
• Best ratio: 4:5 portrait (e.g. 800×1000px)
• Keep each photo under 500KB for fast loading
• Free compression: https://squoosh.app
• Want more than 3 photos? Open index.html, find the "Captured Moments"
  section, and duplicate one of the <div class="g-item">...</div> blocks,
  changing the file name and alt text.

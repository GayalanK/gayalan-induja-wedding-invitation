The Sacred Schedule • திருமண நிகழ்ச்சி நிரல்

Wedding Agenda

09:45 A.M.
Arrival of Groom
மாப்பிள்ளை வரவேற்பு

The groom arrives in procession, welcomed with nadaswaram, kolam, and auspicious blessings from elders.

09:55 A.M.
Arrival of Bride
மணப்பெண் வரவேற்பு

A moment that takes every breath away — the radiant bride makes her grand entrance, adorned in silk and gold, welcomed with rose petals and nadaswaram.

10:10 A.M.
Thali Kattuthal
தாலி கட்டுதல் • மங்கல நாண்

The most sacred moment — the groom ties the holy Thali (mangalsutra) around the bride's neck, uniting their souls for eternity. Please be present to bless this divine union.

10:30 A.M.
Pani Grahanam
பாணி கிரகணம்

The groom takes the bride's right hand in his, symbolising his promise to be her lifelong companion and protector.

10:45 A.M.
Sapthapathi
சப்தபதி

The couple takes seven sacred steps together around the holy fire, making seven vows — health, strength, prosperity, happiness, children, harmony, and eternal friendship.

10:55 A.M.
Arundhathi Paarthal
அருந்ததி பார்த்தல்

The couple gazes at Arundhathi — the star of fidelity — as a pledge of their unwavering devotion to each other through all lifetimes.

11:30 A.M.
Thirumana Sambhurthayangal
திருமண சம்பூர்த்தியங்கள்

Exchange of garlands, gifts, and blessings. Family elders shower the couple with flowers, sweets, and heartfelt wishes.

11:45 A.M.
Wedding Lunch
விருந்து

A traditional Tamil feast served on banana leaves. Please join us to savour authentic cuisine prepared with love and warmth.

02:30 P.M.
Dance Floor Opening
நடன வேடிக்கை • கொண்டாட்டம்

Let the celebrations begin! The newlyweds take the first dance as the floor opens to family and friends — music, joy, and memories in the making.

03:30 P.M.
Leaving the Hall
நிகழ்வு நிறைவு

As the celebrations draw to a close, we bid a warm farewell to our beloved guests. Thank you for being part of our most cherished day.

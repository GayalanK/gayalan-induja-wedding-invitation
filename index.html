The Sacred Schedule • திருமண நிகழ்ச்சி நிரல்

Wedding Agenda

8:00 AM
விநாயகர் பூஜைு
Ganesh Prayer
Invoking Lord Ganesha's blessings to remove obstacles and begin the ceremony auspiciously.
8:20 AM
காப்பு கட்டுதல்
Sacred Thread Tying
A protective thread is tied on the wrists of the bride and groom to ward off evil and bless the union.
8:45 AM
மாப்பிள்ளை அழைப்பு
Groom's Procession
The groom is ceremonially welcomed to the mandapam with nadaswaram music and traditional rituals.
9:05 AM
பெண் அழைப்பு
Bride's Procession
The bride is ceremonially escorted into the mandapam, welcomed with music and blessings before joining the groom.
9:25 AM
கன்னிகாதானம்
Giving Away of the Bride
The bride's father formally places her hand in the groom's, entrusting her to his care.
09:45 AM
பாணிக்கிரகணம்
Taking of the Bride's Hand
The groom accepts the bride's hand, vowing to walk life's journey together.
10:00 AM
அக்னி பிரதிஷ்டை
Sacred Fire Lighting
The sacred fire is lit and invoked as the divine witness to the marriage.
10:10 AM
தாலி கட்டுதல்
Thali Kattuthal
The groom ties the sacred thaali around the bride's neck - the most auspicious moment of the wedding, marking their union.
10:45 AM
சப்தபதி
Sapthapathi
Seven sacred steps taken together, each one a vow for the life ahead.
10:55 AM
அருந்ததி பார்தல்
Arundhathi Paarthal
The couple looks to the star Arundhathi, a symbol of marital devotion and steadfastness.
11:30 AM
ஆசீர்வாதம்
Blessing Ceremony
Elders and guests bless the newlyweds, marking the joyous conclusion of the ceremony.
11:45 AM
விருந்து
Wedding Lunch
Join us for a traditional feast, shared with family and friends.
2:30 PM
கொண்டாட்டம்
Celebration & Dance
Music, dance, and joy - celebrating the newlyweds late into the afternoon.
3:30 PM
நிகழ்வு நிறைவு
Leaving the Hall
The couple departs, carrying with them a lifetime of blessings from everyone here today.
